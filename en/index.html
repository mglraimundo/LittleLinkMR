Dr. Miguel Raimundo

Ophthalmologist at ULS Coimbra and Unidade de Oftalmologia de Coimbra (UOC).

Ophthalmologist MD specializing in cataract and anterior segment surgery, including complex cataract surgery, paediatric cataract, intraocular lens calculations, as well as neuro-ophthalmology.

Coordinator of the Portuguese Lens & Refractive Surgery Group (CIRP) of the Portuguese Society of Ophthalmology for the 2025-2026 biennium.

Collaborates in the development of the intraocular lens calculator of the European Society of Cataract and Refractive Surgeons (ESCRS IOL Calculator), used daily by thousands of surgeons worldwide.

Has a YouTube channel about cataract and anterior segment surgery. Developed a set of tools for intraocular lens calculation (BiomAPI, BiomPEDI and BiomCYL).

Winner of multiple Ophthalmology awards, author of numerous national and international scientific publications, with regular participation in national and international specialty conferences.
